The Golden Bear Inn was a 44-room hotel on San Pablo Avenue. In 2021 the City of Berkeley submitted a joint Homekey application with Bay Area Community Services (BACS) and Memar to convert the property to permanent supportive housing for chronically homeless households. The project reached full occupancy in January 2023. Services are provided by BACS.
